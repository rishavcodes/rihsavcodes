WELCOME!

Hi! I’m rishavcodes.

Coder. Youtuber. Engineer.

I’m a 20 year old Computer Science Student living in Gurugram. I am a python developer at my own freelance company with 1+ years of professional experience. I am currently doing my bachelor of computer science in K R Mangalam University and took coursework in python, web development, business, and computer science. I’ve found it’s not easy being a multi minority in the tech industry. To too many people, these three things define me and I’m on a mission to prove them wrong.

Through a little series on my page called #ReactResiliently, my goal is to help aspiring women in tech (and other groups too, of course!) feel empowered to make their voices heard and equip them with strategies to resiliently handle difficult situations

As I share my personal experiences and learnings, I want to use this platform to document my own journey overcoming the obstacles. My hope is that someone else might be able to better their own life by applying some of this content. At the end of the day, the members of our community need to know that no matter how isolating a circumstance may feel, no one is alone.
